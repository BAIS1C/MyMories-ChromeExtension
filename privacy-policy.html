# Privacy Policy - MyMories **Effective Date:** January 16, 2025 **Last Updated:** January 16, 2025 ## Overview MyMories by Metafintek.com is a Chrome extension that helps users backup and compress their AI chat conversations. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information. ## Data Collection **We collect NO personal data, chat content, or user information.** MyMories operates with a privacy-first approach: - ✅ **Zero data collection** - No personal information is gathered - ✅ **Local processing only** - All compression happens on your device - ✅ **No external transmission** - Your chats never leave your computer - ✅ **No tracking or analytics** - We don't monitor usage or behavior ## How MyMories Works 1. **On-demand activation** - Only runs when you click the extension 2. **Local extraction** - Reads visible chat content from your browser tab 3. **Local compression** - Processes conversations using built-in algorithms 4. **Local storage** - Saves compressed files directly to your Downloads folder 5. **No transmission** - Nothing is sent to external servers ## Permissions Explained ### activeTab Permission - **Purpose:** Read chat content from LLM websites (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) - **Usage:** Only when you explicitly click the extension icon - **Data handling:** Content is processed locally and never transmitted ### downloads Permission - **Purpose:** Save compressed chat files to your Downloads folder - **Usage:** Creates .txt files on your device only - **Data handling:** Files remain on your device under your control ### scripting Permission - **Purpose:** Extract chat messages from webpage DOM - **Usage:** Injects scripts only when extension is activated - **Data handling:** Scripts process content locally and are removed after use ## What We Don't Do - ❌ Store your conversations on external servers - ❌ Transmit your data to third parties - ❌ Use cookies or tracking mechanisms - ❌ Collect personal information or usage statistics - ❌ Share data with advertisers or analytics services - ❌ Access your conversations when extension is not active ## Third-Party Services MyMories does not use any third-party services, APIs, or external dependencies. The extension works completely offline. ## Data Security Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there are no data security concerns related to external storage or transmission. All processing happens locally on your device using your browser's built-in security features. ## Children's Privacy MyMories does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal information from any users, this extension is safe for all age groups. ## International Users MyMories works the same way for all users worldwide - with zero data collection and local-only processing. ## Changes to Privacy Policy We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. ## Your Rights Since we collect no personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You maintain full control over any files created by MyMories on your device. ## Contact Information **Developer:** PT Meta Fin Tek **Website:** https://metafintek.com **Email:** support@metafintek.com For questions about this privacy policy or MyMories functionality, please contact us at the email address above. ## Compliance This privacy policy complies with: - Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies - General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - Other applicable privacy regulations --- **Summary:** MyMories is designed with privacy as the top priority. We collect no data, transmit nothing externally, and process everything locally on your device. Your conversations remain completely private and under your control.
